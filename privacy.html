Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2025

Introduction

Hms5star is a private recruitment agency dedicated to matching the right candidates to the right companies. We rigorously evaluate skills, creativity, and budget requirements so you can focus on your core business. Whether you need short-term support or permanent hires, our responsive consultants ensure you get experienced, hardworking staff under terms that suit your needs.

Our Vision

To partner with organisations and employees, building a progressive community where both thrive in harmony. In a world of constant change, we aim to be your trusted ally for seamless staffing solutions.

Our Values

  • Honesty & Reliability – We stand by our commitments and foster open, trustworthy relationships.
  • Excellence – We deliver flexible, high-quality services tailored to each client’s needs.
  • Integrity – We uphold transparency and consistency in all our dealings.

Ethics & Compliance

  • Legal Compliance – All recruitment processes adhere strictly to applicable laws and regulations.
  • Diversity & Inclusion – We treat every candidate equitably, without regard to race, culture, caste, religion, or any protected characteristic.
  • Confidentiality – We protect client and candidate data in line with GDPR and other privacy standards.

Code of Conduct

All Citywide Personnel employees receive training on our code of conduct, emphasizing professionalism, respect, and community engagement. We partner with local businesses to reduce environmental impact and eliminate single-use plastics throughout our operations.

Sustainability & Environment

We embed eco-friendly practices into every aspect of our work:

  • Tracking, measuring, re-using, and recycling materials to minimize waste.
  • Implementing systems to reduce greenhouse gas emissions in logistics.
  • Continuously exploring innovations that benefit both clients and the planet.

Health & Safety

The well-being of our staff and candidates is paramount. We conduct regular risk assessments, safety tours, and training sessions. Through ongoing audits and KPI monitoring, we maintain a culture where safety is everyone’s responsibility.

Environment Policy

We minimize hazardous effects on human health by enforcing strict waste management and advocating sustainable lifestyle choices within our community.

Manned Guarding Services

Our SIA-approved security officers offer tailored protection for your people, premises, and assets. Supervised by local management, they deliver a personable presence—ideal for public spaces like shopping centres and leisure facilities.
