Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2023

At Conit Recruitment, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our recruitment services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register on our website
  • Submit a job application
  • Sign up for our newsletter
  • Contact us through our contact form
  • Upload your resume or CV

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Employment history, education, and qualifications
  • Professional references and credentials
  • Any other information you choose to provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Match you with suitable employment opportunities
  • Contact you regarding job openings or recruitment updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer service
  • Send you our newsletter and marketing communications
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Potential employers when you apply for a position through our services
  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • Business partners with whom we jointly offer products or services
  • Legal and regulatory authorities when required by law

We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to maintain the security of your personal information. However, no data transmission over the Internet or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure, please immediately notify us.

5. Your Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to processing of your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

7. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
