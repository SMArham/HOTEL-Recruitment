About Us

Get to know the team behind Conit Recruitment

Our Story

Founded in 2007, Conit Recruitment has grown to become a leading hospitality staffing partner for premium hotels and resorts worldwide.

What began as a small recruitment agency in London has evolved into a global operation with offices in major hospitality hubs across Europe, North America, Asia, and the Middle East. Our journey has been driven by a singular vision: to connect exceptional talent with outstanding hospitality organizations.

Throughout our history, we've maintained our commitment to understanding both the unique needs of hospitality employers and the career aspirations of industry professionals. This dual perspective allows us to create perfect matches that drive business success and professional growth.
