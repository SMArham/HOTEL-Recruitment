Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What types of positions do you recruit for?

We specialize in recruiting for a wide range of hospitality positions, from housekeeping and front desk staff to executive management and specialized roles like concierge and security personnel.

How quickly can you provide staff?

Our response time varies based on position requirements and urgency. For standard roles, we typically provide candidates within 1-2 weeks, while specialized positions may take 2-4 weeks. We also offer emergency staffing solutions for urgent needs.

Do you offer temporary or permanent staffing solutions?

We provide both temporary and permanent staffing solutions to meet the diverse needs of hospitality businesses. Our flexible arrangements include temporary staff for seasonal peaks, contract-based positions, and permanent placements for long-term roles.

What is your placement guarantee?

We offer tiered placement guarantees depending on the service package selected. Our standard guarantee is 30 days, while our premium and enterprise packages extend to 60 and 90 days respectively. During this period, if a candidate doesn't meet expectations, we'll provide a replacement at no additional cost.
